about

i’m iggy. i’ve built small ventures — laundry systems, surfboard rentals, and tools for contractors. i’ve helped with space-related contract work in malaysia, and now i’m co-building an underwater robot in kraków. my focus is on creating simple, durable tech — starting with ai tools for divers and expanding toward systems that support blue collar work. no buzzwords. just tools that work.

i’m not building alone. i’m looking for people who like solving problems with their hands and heads. engineers who ship. robotics tinkerers. ai folks who want to work with physical systems, not just screens. i don’t care about degrees — i care if you move fast, think clearly, and tell the truth.

right now: underwater robotics. diver vision tools. human-ai workflows for contractors. beyond that: wherever the signal leads. interests include deep water, high places, tough tech, and making good things with good people. if that resonates — reach out.
